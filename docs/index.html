How to add a calendar to your Google calendar

  1. Right click on a calendar and select "Copy link address"
  2. Open your Google calendar
  3. Click the plus sign next to “Other Calendars” on the left menu
  4. Click "From URL"
  5. Paste your calendar URL
  6. Click "Add calendar"
